About Me

With over a decade of dedicated teaching experience, I am a charismatic and dependable educator committed to empowering students to achieve academic excellence. Specializing in IIT-JEE/NEET Physics, I employ innovative teaching methods, solving 50 questions daily to ensure comprehensive preparation. My expertise extends to IGCSE Math and Physics, along with Cambridge International AS and A Level Physics. I simplify complex concepts using online resources, teaching shortcuts and tricks to enhance problem-solving skills, fostering a results-oriented approach. Currently at Greenvalley International School, Trivandrum, I handle Cambridge International AS and A Level Physics and IGCSE Physics, showcasing my proficiency in delivering quality education.

In the past year, I've successfully conducted virtual classes for Lakes Grammar School in New South Wales, Australia, a student from the British International School, and two students from HLC International School in Chennai, covering IGCSE Physics (8th-12th grade) and Mathematics (8th-10th grade) using electronic slate through Skype and Zoom. Additionally, I bring three years of experience teaching NEET/JEE Physics, both mains and advance classes, at various schools and esteemed institutes. Notably, I have mentored two NEET 2019 rankers (Kerala state 63 & 234) and facilitated seven MBBS selections in NEET 2018 and NEET 2019, reflecting my commitment to student success.
